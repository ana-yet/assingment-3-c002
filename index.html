What is Grocery Shop?

Grocery Shop is an online vegetable grocery store that delivers fresh, high-quality vegetables to your doorstep.

Do you sell organic vegetables?

Yes! We offer a variety of organic and chemical-free vegetables. You can find them in our "Organic" section.

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our website, add vegetables to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can select your preferred delivery time before completing the order.

What are your delivery areas?

We currently deliver to Pakistan. If you're unsure, enter your zip code at checkout to check availability.

How long does delivery take?

We offer same-day or next-day delivery based on your location. You can also schedule a preferred delivery time during checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, UPI, and online wallets.

Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order within 5m by contacting our support team.
